To use this Delivery Routing Optimization tool

Follow the steps as given in the menu starting with - 1. Route and Truck Information After that, continue until Step - 4. In this step enter order information with customer name , Address and Quantity of oil in gallons, System will generate a RANDOM duration for delivery

Then choose Step-5 and click optimize route (Map should also get updated automatically)

If you want you may choose to edit the delivery sequence or delete in Step - 6

If more than 15 locations are specified, you are not guaranteed to get the optimal solution, but the solution is likely to be close to the best possible.
